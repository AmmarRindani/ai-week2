**The Ultimate Exam Preparation Guide for Intellectual Property Law**

**WHAT THIS SUBJECT IS ABOUT**

Intellectual Property Law, also known as IPR, is a crucial subject that deals with the rights and protections for creative works, ideas, and inventions. It's a complex but fascinating area of law that helps people and businesses own and control their original creations. Think of it like this: Intellectual Property Law is the safeguard that secures the value and uniqueness of human innovation!

**KEY DEFINITIONS TO MEMORISE**

Here are the essential terms and their simple definitions:

1. **Intellectual Property (IP)**: Creative works, ideas, and inventions.
2. **Patent**: Exclusive right to make, use, and sell an invention.
3. **Trademark**: Symbol, word, or logo that identifies a brand.
4. **Copyright**: Exclusive right to reproduce, distribute, or display a creative work.
5. **Trade Secret**: Confidential business information.
6. **Industrial Design**: The ornamental appearance of a product.
7. **Geographical Indication (GI)**: Label that indicates a product's origin.
